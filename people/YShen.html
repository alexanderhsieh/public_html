Research interest

My research is at the interface between biology, statistics, and computer science. I focus on developing and applying computational methods to study human genetics and diseases. Specific areas of interest include genome sequencing and assembly, genetic mapping of complex diseases, MHC, autoimmunity, and pharmacogenomics.
